## Paper trading vs live trading

**Paper trading** — Available without a wallet. Simulates trades with no real money. All features work identically. Trades are recorded in your local trade history.

**Live trading** — Requires a connected wallet with USDC on Polygon. Orders are submitted to Polymarket's CLOB (Central Limit Order Book) and signed by your wallet via Para.

## Trade simulation

From the AI chat or the Trade dashboard:

1. The AI recommends a trade with a trade card
2. Click **Simulate** to open the simulation modal
3. Review: market, side (YES/NO), shares, entry price, cost, potential payout
4. Adjust shares if needed
5. Click **Confirm** to record the paper trade

## Live execution

1. Connect your wallet
2. Navigate to the Execute page or confirm from a trade card
3. Select a market, side, and shares
4. Review the order book and current spread
5. Click **Execute** — your wallet signs the EIP-712 order
6. The order is submitted to Polymarket's CLOB
7. Fill status updates in real-time

## Edge scoring

The Trade dashboard scores every market with the edge engine:

- **STRONG** — High volume, tight spread, significant movement. Best execution conditions.
- **MODERATE** — Decent liquidity, some movement. Tradeable with caution.
- **WEAK** — Thin liquidity or wide spread. High slippage risk.

## Signals

Each market shows honest signals from the order book:
- Spread width (tight vs wide)
- Activity level (high vs low volume)
- Price movement (trending vs stable)
- Liquidity depth
- Time to resolution
