## Overview

The Execute page is the order terminal — direct access to Polymarket's CLOB for placing limit orders with full order book visibility.

## Order book

The live order book shows bid and ask prices with depth. Updates every 3 seconds.

- **Asks** (red) — prices sellers are willing to accept
- **Bids** (green) — prices buyers are willing to pay
- **Spread** — difference between best ask and best bid

A tight spread means better execution. A wide spread means higher slippage.

## Placing an order

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a market">
    Search for a market by name. The order book loads automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose side and shares">
    Select YES or NO. Enter the number of shares. The cost and potential payout calculate automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review signals">
    Check the edge score and signals before confirming. STRONG = good conditions, WEAK = high slippage risk.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Execute">
    Click Execute. Your wallet signs the EIP-712 order. Fill status updates in real-time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Bankroll management

Set your total bankroll on the Execute page. Kuroko uses this to:
- Calculate position sizing as a percentage of bankroll
- Warn when a single trade exceeds 10% of bankroll
- Show category exposure breakdown in Portfolio

## Fill tracking

After submitting an order, the fill status updates every 3 seconds:
- **Pending** — order submitted, waiting for fill
- **Filled** — order fully executed
- **Partial** — partially filled
- **Cancelled** / **Rejected** — order not executed

## Paper mode

Without a wallet, all orders are paper trades. The full flow works identically — you see the same order book, signals, and fill tracking, but no real money moves.
