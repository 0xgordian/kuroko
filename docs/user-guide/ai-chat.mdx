## Overview

The AI chat is the primary interface in Kuroko. It's powered by the aomi SDK and has live Polymarket market data injected into every message — so the AI always knows current prices, volumes, and movements without you having to paste anything.

## What the AI knows

On every message, the AI receives:

- Top 10 markets by volume with current YES probability, 24h change, volume, and liquidity
- Biggest movers in the last 24 hours
- Your open positions (if wallet connected)

## What you can ask

**Market analysis**
- "What's the best trade right now?"
- "Analyze the Fed rate cut market — is there edge here?"
- "What markets have moved the most today?"

**Portfolio**
- "Show me my open positions"
- "Which positions should I exit?"
- "What's my total P&L?"

**Actions**
- "Set an alert when Bitcoin ETF approval hits 80%"
- "Set a stop-loss at 30% on my largest position"
- "Take me to the order terminal"

## AI-triggered actions

The AI can directly control the UI. When you ask it to set an alert, create a guard, or navigate to a page — it does it automatically. You'll see a toast notification confirming the action.

## Trade cards

When the AI recommends a trade, it renders an interactive trade card inline:

```
KUROKO          [PAPER TRADE]
TRADE RECORD

| Will the Fed cut rates in June?

SIDE          SHARES
YES           90

ENTRY PRICE   COST
44¢           $39.60

IF CORRECT    RETURN
$90.00        +127%
```

Click **Execute Trade** to confirm, or **Review** to see more details.

## Slash commands

Type `/` to open the command palette. Select a command to pre-fill the input — you can edit before sending.

## Voice input

Click the microphone icon. Speak your query. The transcript appears in the input and sends automatically on final result.
