## Overview

The Markets page shows all active Polymarket markets with live data. Use it to discover opportunities, set alerts, and navigate to specific markets for analysis.

## Filtering

**Category filter** — Filter by topic: Elections, Crypto, Sports, Economics, Science, Entertainment, or All.

**Search** — Full-text search across all market questions. Results update as you type.

**Sort** — Sort by volume, probability, or 24h change.

**Probability range** — Filter to markets within a specific probability range (e.g. 40-60% for near-50% markets).

## Market cards

Each card shows:
- Market question
- Current YES probability
- 24h probability change (green = up, red = down)
- Volume
- Liquidity
- End date

## Actions

**Analyze** — Opens the AI chat with the market pre-loaded for analysis.

**Set Alert** — Creates a price alert for this market directly from the card.

## Live data

Market data refreshes every 2 minutes from Polymarket's Gamma API. The top 60 markets also get 7-day and 30-day price history from the CLOB API.
