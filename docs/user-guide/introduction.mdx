## Overview

Kuroko is an AI-powered web app that helps you trade on [Polymarket](https://polymarket.com) — the world's largest prediction market platform.

Instead of watching 1,000+ markets manually, Kuroko's AI watches them for you. It surfaces opportunities, explains the reasoning, and can execute trades through your connected wallet.

## Two modes

**Co-pilot mode** — You're in control. Ask the AI questions, get analysis, confirm trades yourself.

**Autopilot mode** — The agent runs in the background, finds opportunities, and proposes trades. You approve or set auto-execute rules.

## Paper trading

Kuroko works without a wallet. All features are available in paper-trade mode — simulated trades with no real money. Connect a wallet when you're ready to go live.
