## Why connect a wallet?

Kuroko works in paper-trade mode without a wallet. Connecting a wallet enables:

- Live order execution on Polymarket (Polygon network)
- AI sees your open positions and factors them into analysis
- Position guards auto-execute when triggered
- Real P&L tracking

## How to connect

Click **Connect Wallet** in the top navigation bar. This opens the Para wallet modal.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose your login method">
    Select Google, Twitter, Discord, GitHub, or enter your email. No seed phrase required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete authentication">
    Follow the OAuth flow. Para creates a non-custodial wallet tied to your social account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wallet connected">
    Your wallet address appears in green in the top nav. The AI now has access to your positions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing your wallet

Click your wallet address (shown in green in the nav) to open the Para account management modal. From there you can:

- View your balance
- Add funds
- Send or withdraw
- Disconnect

## About Para

Kuroko uses [Para](https://getpara.com) for wallet management. Para is a non-custodial wallet SDK — your keys are never stored by Kuroko or Para's servers. Signing happens locally in your browser.

## Network

All Polymarket trades execute on **Polygon** (MATIC). Make sure you have USDC on Polygon to fund live trades.
