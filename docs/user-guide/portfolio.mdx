## Overview

The Portfolio page has four tabs: Positions, Alerts, Guards, and Trade History.

## Positions

Shows your open Polymarket positions when a wallet is connected. Each position displays:
- Market question
- Side (YES/NO)
- Shares held
- Current probability
- Unrealized P&L
- Entry price

## Alerts

Price alerts notify you when a market crosses a threshold.

**Creating an alert:**
1. Click **New Alert** or ask the AI: "Set an alert when X hits 70%"
2. Select the market, condition (above/below), and threshold
3. Enable browser notifications when prompted

Alerts check every 60 seconds against live market data.

## Position Guards

Stop-loss and take-profit rules that auto-execute when triggered.

**Creating a guard:**
1. Click **New Guard** or ask the AI: "Set a stop-loss at 30% on my position"
2. Select the market, trigger condition, and shares to exit
3. Optionally set both a stop-loss and take-profit

When a guard triggers:
- **With wallet connected** — Attempts a live exit order through aomi → Para signing
- **Without wallet** — Records a paper trade and fires a browser notification

Guards deactivate after triggering to prevent re-execution.

## Trade History

Complete log of all paper and live trades with:
- Market, side, shares, price
- Total cost
- Outcome (if resolved)
- P&L
- CSV export
